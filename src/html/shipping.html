Shipping & Delivery

If you place your order Monday through Friday before 3pm, your order willship the same day. You will receive a tracking number the day that your order ships. In some cases, if your order needs extra clarification or customization it may take up to 3-4 business days for processing and verification.If you place an order on the weekend, your order will be processed during working hours. If you require delivery by a specific date please contact us writing to help@onrial.com prior to placing your order

Estimated Delivery Cost

All of our products are sent from Poland warehouse, handled as international shipping. We try our best to make the delivery cost as low aspossible.

How do I track my order?

Each order is sent with a tracking number that you will receive in an emailshortly after your order has fulfilled. Please click the tracking number provided in the body of your email. You can also enter your tracking number at given URL of delivery site. If for some reason you have not received the email with tracking number, it is possible that the email address you provided was incorrect or the email has gone to your spam folder. Please run a search for the email, if you can't find the email, feel free to contact us, we are happy to help!

My order was delivered to a wrong address

If your order was delivered to a wrong address due to our or shipping company's fault, we will send your original ordered products ASAP at our shipping cost and will provide you with our DHL account so that you can return the wrong products to us. If your order was delivered to a wrong address due to your own mistake(such as typing wrong shipping address), regrettably we will not be responsible for the extra shipping cost and involves rectifying your order.

We recommend you contact the shipping company with your original tracking number and arrange delivery to your correct address. Please note that once products leave our warehouse, it is extremely difficult to change the address of the recipient, so please double check your shipping address before completing your order.

Missing or Lost Packages

Is your order missing or wondering where it is? We are SO sorry for any inconvenience! When your order shipped we sent you an email with tracking information for your order. We recommend checking this trackinglink.
