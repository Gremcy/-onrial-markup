THE DROP EXTENDS

  • REASON

    HOW TO SOLVE THE problem

  • The drop did not change for more than 25 minutes

    Refresh the droplet

  • The glue is open for more than 3-4 weeks

    Replace the glue with a fresh one. After opening, the shelf life of the glue is 1-2 months and depends on the intensity of use, storage temperature and season

  • There is high humidity near the drop of glue

    Eliminate the cause of high humidity (the cause may be a damp cotton pad lying nearby, a cup of tea, etc.) Provide optimal humidity in the work area

  • The glue was not shaken before use

    Shake well before use

  • The cap on the tube was not closed well enough, the glue was stored incorrectly

    Replace the glue with fresh glue and follow the recommendations for storing the glue

  • The glue is taken from the bottom of the working drop

    Practice the technique of working with a drop

  • There is a large flow of air in your work area (draft, air conditioner)

    Use a booster from the Onrial company and your drop will not thicken

BUNDLES COLLAPSE

  • REASON

    HOW TO SOLVE THE problem

  • Incorrectly selected glue speed, slow glue corrodes

    Replace the glue witha faster one, treat the eyelashes with a primer, use a booster

  • A large amount of glue on the bundle

    Do not immerse the leg very deeply in the drop, and do not quickly remove it from the drop

  • The humidity in the room has decreased

    Make a longer expoSure wne attaching an artificial eyelash to a natural eyelash, use a humidifier

  • Incompatibility of materials

    Whenever possible, use materials of the same brand

POOR WEARABILITY

  • REASON

    HOW TO SOLVE THE problem

  • The master cant keep up with the glue

    Use an adhesive with a lower setting speed

  • Poorly prepared eyelashes before extensions

    Follow the technology of the extension procedure, carry out high-quality cleaning of eyelashes before extension

  • When attaching an artificial eyelash to a natural eyelash, the master could not withstand the collision area

    Follow the building technology do not rush

  • Excess glue with artificial eyelashes was smeared

    Follow proper drop technique

  • Non-optimal temperature and humidity, impurities in the air (evaporation from wax melt for depilation, evaporation from ammonia paint)

    Monitor the temperature and humidity, do not carry out the procedure if the conditions are not optimal

  • A nebulizer was used, accelerated polymerization occurred

    Do not use glue polymerization enhancers, do not disrupt the natural hardening process

  • The client got caught in the rain after the procedure

    Warn the client about possible bad eyelash wear

  • Excessive alcohol consumption before or after the procedure, hormonal

    Warn the client about possible bad eyelash wear

FAST DRYING GLUE

  • REASON

    HOW TO SOLVE THE problem

  • It is very hot indoors

    Create optimal conditions

  • A very small drop of glue is used

    Follow the technique of working with a drop

  • High humidity indoors

    Create optimal conditions

THE GLUE STOPPED WORKING

  • REASON

    HOW TO SOLVE THE problem

  • Change in temperature or humidity in the room

    Monitor the optimal temperature

  • There is not enough oxygen in the room

    Ventilate the room

A FEW MORE TIPS

  • Keep in your arsenal several types of glue with different adhesion speeds, in case of problems with the main glue, you can finish the procedure by using an alternative
  • Pay attention to the extension technique often, it primarily affects the term of eyelash wear
  • After opening, change the glue at least once every 2 months, even if it has not been used much
